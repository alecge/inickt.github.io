Freelance Jobs

On request I can provide freelance graphic design work. I can do anything from posters to logos, flyers or handouts. All of the Peotone Farmer's Market posters and the Flossmoor Green Committee's Bag the Habit poster were created for use in our local communities. Although I am a student and still learning, I will be able to help you create a design that fits your needs for an affordable price. Contact me for more information for your specific needs.
